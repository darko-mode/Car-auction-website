About AutoBid

Connecting car enthusiasts with exceptional vehicles through transparent, trusted auctions

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by a team of automotive enthusiasts, AutoBid was born from a simple observation: the traditional car buying and selling experience was broken. Whether you were a collector looking for that perfect classic, an enthusiast seeking a weekend driver, or someone ready to part with a beloved vehicle, the process was often frustrating, opaque, and inefficient.

We set out to create something different – a platform that would bring transparency, trust, and excitement back to automotive transactions. By focusing exclusively on enthusiast and collector vehicles, we've built a community where passion meets commerce, and every auction tells a story.

Today, AutoBid has facilitated over $500 million in vehicle sales, connecting thousands of buyers and sellers across the country. From rare classics to modern supercars, from weekend project cars to daily drivers with character, we celebrate the diversity of automotive passion.

Trust & Transparency
